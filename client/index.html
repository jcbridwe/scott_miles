Bio

Scott Miles has been a Virginia Lawyer for the past 22 years, serving as a legal aid staff attorney, then public defender, then prosecutor, and now private practitioner. He grew up on a beef cattle farm in Albemarle County, went to William & Mary on an ROTC Scholarship, and served as an Army Officer in the Persian Gulf War and Somalia before attending law school. He lives with his wife, two children, two dogs, and six goats on Belmont Road in Chesterfield County. He works out of his office on Iron Bridge Road, and regularly appears in Chesterfield County Courts.
